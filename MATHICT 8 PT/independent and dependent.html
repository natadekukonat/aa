Independent and Dependent Variables

























Variable

A variable is an alphabet or term that represents an unknown number,

unknown value, or unknown quantity.








Independent Variable

An independent variable is a variable that does not depend on any other variable for its value. It
may also refer as the input of the function.

Dependent Variable

A dependent variable is a variable in an expression that depends on the value of another variable.
It is affected by the changes on the independent variable. It may also refer as the output of the
function.
